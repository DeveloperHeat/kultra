Our Vision for Kultrà

Our business idea is to share our love for cultures across the world through food. And we’ve chosen chocolate as our tool. We’ll use a New Zealand-made chocolate base and incorporate flavours, toppings, and elements that tell unique stories from different regions around the globe. Each flavour will represent a special moment or cultural experience, connecting people across land and ocean. Our vision is to offer a range of handcrafted chocolate bars, where every flavour is a journey. Our mission is to make the world feel more connected—one flavour at a time. Our values include sustainability, cultural respect, and collaboration. We’re committed to using local ingredients, partnering with regional suppliers, and ensuring our brand aligns with both environmental and cultural responsibility.

Our Upcoming Offerings

Coffee with Almond (Whittaker’s Milk Chocolate Base)

This flavour captures the essence of South America with its robust coffee - a staple crop in Brazil and Colombia, known worldwide for its richness and aroma. The almonds offer a nutty crunch, often enjoyed in South American desserts. Paired with Whittaker’s Milk Chocolate, this bar pays homage to the region’s deep coffee culture and agricultural diversity.

Chill with Mango (Whittaker’s Milk Chocolate Base)

Inspired by African flavour profiles, this chocolate brings together the heat of African chilli peppers – common in North and West African cooking – with the tropical sweetness of mango, widely grown across East Africa. The combination reflects the continent’s vibrant cuisine, where bold spices and fresh fruits often collide in unforgettable ways.

Matcha with Raspberry (Whittaker’s White Chocolate Base)

This limited-edition bar draws on Japan’s ceremonial-grade matcha, a symbol of East Asian tradition, tranquillity, and craftsmanship. Combined with tart raspberry – a nod to the growing fusion of Western fruits in East Asian desserts – it celebrates the balance of ancient tea rituals and modern patisserie. The white chocolate base enhances the elegance of the blend.
